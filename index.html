📂 SD Card Setup Guide

1
Format SD card as FAT32 or exFAT
2
Create platform folders — e.g., /roms/gba/, /roms/nes/
3
Copy ROM files into the matching platform folder
4
Insert SD & boot — EmuELEC will scan and list your games
